About Tan Pro

Tan Pro USA opened its doors in April 1998, starting with a 30 bed mega store in Mansfield Ohio. The concept was simple; build large clean facilities, offer no-appointment tanning, outfit the store with state of the art equipment, and use a comprehensive innovative marketing plan. The store was one of the first truly mega stores located in the State of Ohio and was used regularly by equipment manufacturers to showcase the future of indoor tanning. Today Tan Pro is currently Ohio’s largest chain with 44 locations throughout Ohio and is committed to maintaining that status. Tan Pro has continued its stellar growth through Tan Pro’s creative marketing plans, superior customer service and excellent home office support and training.
